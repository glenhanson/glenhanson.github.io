The Gap I Fill

You need senior technical leadership. Someone who can architect production ML systems, guide product strategy around AI, and translate between engineering teams and the business. But you don't need (or can't justify) a full-time hire.

That's where I come in. I embed directly into your team as a fractional ML engineer, tech lead, head of product, or whatever the role needs to be. I bring the engineering depth to build real systems and the business judgment to decide what's worth building.

Most engagements start in one place and expand as the work reveals what's actually needed. That's by design. I'm not here to deliver a scoped project and disappear. I'm here to fill a leadership gap and help you build the right thing.

How I Work

I don't hand off slide decks or prototypes that sit on a shelf. Everything I build goes into production.

I Embed in Your Team

I work alongside your engineers, not above them. I join standups, review PRs, and build alongside the team while also stepping back to think about architecture, strategy, and what's next.

I Build Production Systems

ML models, data pipelines, APIs, infrastructure. I design, build, and deploy with AWS CDK, Docker, and CI/CD. This isn't notebook-and-a-prayer data science. It's engineering.

I Shape Strategy

I inform roadmaps, identify what's technically feasible, scope what will actually move the needle, and help leadership make better decisions about where to invest in data and AI.

What This Looks Like

Every engagement is different. Here's the pattern.

Fractional ML Engineer

Building the ML that powers the product

Serving as the sole ML engineer on a product team, designing, building, and deploying the machine learning systems behind a SaaS platform, end to end from research through production infrastructure.

Fractional Head of Product

Guiding a product roadmap with deep domain expertise

Blending technical engineering skills with specialized domain knowledge to shape the product direction, build backend infrastructure, and drive data R&D.

Fractional Tech Lead

Stepping up when the project needs more than a data scientist

Brought on for data science work, then stepped into a broader leadership role. Designing production systems, leading the engineering team, and identifying new revenue opportunities.

Fractional COO

Bridging a founder's vision and product execution

A non-technical founder needed someone who could translate their domain expertise into a product roadmap. Prioritizing features, managing resources, and driving a product to market.
